❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour package? ▼
You can book by filling out our enquiry form, calling us at +91 98765 43210, or visiting our office. Our travel expert will get in touch, understand your preferences, and share a customised itinerary with pricing. Once confirmed, a 25% advance secures your booking.
What is your cancellation policy? ▼
Cancellations made 30+ days before travel receive an 80% refund. 15-30 days: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: no refund. Group tours may have different policies. Please read the terms on the booking confirmation for full details.
Do you offer customized itineraries? ▼
Absolutely! All our packages can be fully customized. Just let us know your preferred destinations, budget, travel dates, and travel style — and we'll craft a bespoke itinerary just for you.
How long does visa processing take? ▼
Visa processing times vary by country. Some like Thailand and Bali offer visa on arrival. UAE and Singapore take 3-7 working days. UK, USA, and Australia can take 15-45 working days. We recommend applying well in advance.
Are flights included in your packages? ▼
Most packages are quoted without airfare unless explicitly mentioned. We can assist with flight bookings at competitive rates. International packages often include flight options — please check the specific package details or ask our team.
Do you provide travel insurance? ▼
We strongly recommend travel insurance for all trips, especially international ones. We can assist in getting you covered through our trusted insurance partners. Please ask our team for options and pricing.
