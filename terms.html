A minimum advance payment of 25% of the total tour cost is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance must be paid at least 15 days prior to the date of travel. For international tours, the balance must be cleared 30 days prior.

3. Cancellation & Refund Policy

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:
